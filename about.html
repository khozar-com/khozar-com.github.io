Welcome to our Clinic

We are a fully equipped private surgery and casualty within RH Matjhabeng Hospital, which was formerly know as Ernest Oppenheimer Hospital. This surgery and casualty is manned by experienced doctors in medical practice and provides quality services such as free male medical circumcisions, HIV management and occupational health. The medical facilities use a multi-disciplinary approach to patient management to ensure that majority of health requirements can be dealt with.
